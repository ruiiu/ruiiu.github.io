My name is Rui, currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, working with Prof. Pratap Tokekar. I got my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I work at the intersection of robotics, machine learning, reinforcement learning and computer vision. Specifically, I focus on enabling robots to acquire new skills from human or environmental demonstrations, allowing them to adapt to different tasks and environments without complex manual programming. The ultimate goal is to leverage these technologies to enhance the quality of human life.
